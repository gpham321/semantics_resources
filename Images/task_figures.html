Draft caption

Figure 2.

Our manipulation suite comprises seven teleoperated tasks on an xArm: placing a mug on a single-serve coffee maker, pouring a cup into a bowl, hanging a mug on a tree, inserting a power brick into a drawer, setting a power drill on a mouse pad, placing a bottle/can on a coaster, and turning off a faucet. Each task is collected over a grid of object variations (the config chip; e.g. 4×4 = 4 cups × 4 machines) and contributes [N] demonstrations. Average teleoperation time per demonstration ranges from ~11 s (coaster) to ~35 s (pour), with a suite mean of ~25 s. Backgrounds are removed for clarity; raw captures appear in the supplement.
