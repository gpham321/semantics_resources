Design system & decisions

  • Type — Arial (Liberation Sans, its metric-compatible twin, when rendered on Linux) for all figure text — a clean, neutral sans that reads cleanly at small sizes in print.
  • In- vs out-of-distribution — cool panel for in-distribution, warm panel for held-out.
  • Feature highlights — mug clearance zone on machines (translucent green, detail shows through); handle geometry called out on cups. One annotated exemplar per category; the rest stay clean.
  • Backgrounds — every object on white / transparent, so the figure drops onto any page.
  • Geometry — absolute sizes & dimensions live in the caption (drafted below), per your plan; the figure carries shape/handle/clearance cues only.
